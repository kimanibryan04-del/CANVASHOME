Privacy

At Canvas Homes, your privacy is a top priority. This policy details how we collect, use, and protect your personal information, reflecting our commitment to maintaining the highest standards of data security and transparency.

  1. Home
  2. Privacy

Last updated: January 10, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us provide a more personalized and efficient real estate experience. This includes details you provide when inquiring about properties, as well as data that helps us improve our website functionality.

Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • E-mail
  • Address

Usage Data

Technical information regarding your interaction with our site to help us optimize your browsing experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to facilitate property transactions, respond to inquiries, and enhance our service offerings. Our primary goal is to provide you with a seamless and secure real estate journey.

Primary Uses

  1. Facilitating Transactions: We use your information to manage property inquiries, coordinate viewings, and ensure a seamless experience throughout the buying, selling, or rental process.
  2. Personalized Communication: Your data allows us to respond to your specific requests and provide tailored property recommendations based on your unique preferences.
  3. Service Optimization: We analyze usage data to improve our website’s functionality and enhance the digital experience for all Canvas Homes users.
  4. Security and Compliance: Information is utilized to maintain the security of our platform and ensure all interactions meet our professional and legal standards.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except to provide the services you have requested.

We Never Sell Your Data

Your trust is our most valuable asset. At Canvas Homes, we maintain a strict policy of never selling, trading, or otherwise transferring your personal information to outside parties. We believe your data belongs to you, and we use it exclusively to enhance your real estate experience and provide the services you have requested directly from us.

Data Security

We implement a variety of advanced security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Our platform utilizes industry-standard encryption and secure server protocols to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your data. We are committed to ensuring that your digital footprint remains secure throughout your entire journey with Canvas Homes.

Encryption

We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your data during transmission, ensuring that your sensitive information remains private and secure.

Secure Storage

Your data is stored in high-security environments with restricted access, protecting your personal details from unauthorized disclosure or loss.

Your Rights

You have the right to manage your personal data. We believe in transparency and provide you with the tools necessary to view, update, or request the deletion of your information at any time.

Right to Access

You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you to ensure its accuracy and understand how it is being utilized by our team.

Right to Rectification

If you believe any information we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request an immediate correction to ensure your records are up to date.

Right to Erasure

Also known as the 'right to be forgotten,' you may request the deletion of your personal data from our systems when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze site traffic, personalize your experience, and improve our services. These small data files help us remember your preferences and understand how you interact with our platform to provide a smoother browsing journey.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential These cookies are strictly necessary to provide you with services available through our website and to use some of its features, such as access to secure areas. Session
Analytics Used to collect information about how visitors use our site. We use the data to compile reports and help us improve the overall website experience. 2 Years
Marketing Used to track visitors across websites. The intention is to display ads that are relevant and engaging for the individual user and thereby more valuable for publishers. 1 Year

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the 'Last Updated' date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out to our dedicated privacy team.
